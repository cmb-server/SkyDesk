Free airport radar forever. Pro features include a 7-day free trial.

SkyDesk is a browser extension for Google Chrome™ that shows live aircraft (ADS-B) flight radar right on the page you are viewing. It provides an on-page corner overlay plus a full radar tab, displaying real-time plane blips with callsign, altitude, speed, aircraft type, flight routes (origin/destination), and nearby airport information.

What SkyDesk does

  • On-page radar overlay that follows you while you browse, plus a dedicated full radar tab.
  • Live aircraft positions from public ADS-B data, with callsign, altitude, speed, and aircraft type tags.
  • Flight routes (origin and destination) and flight/tail tracking on a world map.
  • Airport information: runways, Class B/C airspace, ground mode, and airport markers.
  • Optional weather, terrain, and water layers, emergency squawk highlighting, and coordinate/location centering.
